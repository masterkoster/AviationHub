Everything in one cockpit

Built for pilots who actually fly

Logbook, planning, currency, and aircraft management — unified in one fast desktop app. No subscriptions for the basics. No cloud lock-in.

Pilot Logbook

Full time breakdowns — PIC, SIC, night, instrument, cross-country. Search and filter your entire flight history.

Interactive Map

Explore 20,000+ airports with fuel prices, frequencies, and runway info. Plan routes visually with waypoints.

Weight & Balance

Built-in W&B calculator with CG visualization. Pre-flight planning made simple and visual.

Currency Tracking

FAA currency rules computed from your logbook — night landings, IPC, BFR, medical. Always know your status.

Fuel Planning

Compare fuel prices, calculate range, and find the cheapest stops along your route.

Route Weather

METAR, TAF, and wind aloft for your entire route. See the fuel impact of headwinds.

Offline-first & Private

Works entirely offline. Your data stays on your machine — no account required, no cloud lock-in.

Encrypted Backups

Export and import your data with .ahb encrypted backup files. Your logbook, portable and safe.

Keyboard-first

Ctrl+1–8 for rapid navigation, Ctrl+K command palette. Power-user workflows, built in.

Your data, your call

Two ways to run it

Default

Local Mode

Data stays on your machine. No account required. Works completely offline. Protected with a PIN. This is the default — most pilots never need anything else.

Optional

Cloud Mode

Sign in to sync across devices. Access your logbook from the desktop app and, eventually, the web. Sync is opt-in — never mandatory.

On the horizon

Web version coming soon

A browser-based experience is in development. For now, grab the Windows desktop app — it's fully offline and your data never leaves your machine unless you choose to sync.
